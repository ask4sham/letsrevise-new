Cell Structure

Explore the microscopic world of animal and plant cells

🔬 Interactive Diagrams 📚 Organelle Functions 🎯 Quick Quiz

Cells are the basic building blocks of all living organisms. They work like tiny factories, each with specialized parts called organelles that perform specific functions.

🐭 Animal Cell

Key Organelles:

Nucleus Control Center
Contains DNA and controls all cell activities
Mitochondria Powerhouse
Produces energy (ATP) through cellular respiration
Ribosomes Protein Factory
Site of protein synthesis
Cell Membrane Gatekeeper
Controls what enters and leaves the cell

🌿 Plant Cell

Plant-Specific Organelles:

Chloroplasts Food Factory
Contains chlorophyll for photosynthesis
Cell Wall Support
Made of cellulose, provides structure and support
Large Vacuole Storage
Stores cell sap, helps maintain cell shape
Plasmodesmata Channels
Allows communication between plant cells

📊 Cell Comparison

Feature Animal Cell Plant Cell
Cell Wall ❌ Absent ✅ Present (cellulose)
Chloroplasts ❌ Absent ✅ Present (for photosynthesis)
