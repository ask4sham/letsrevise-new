Inheritance, Variation & Evolution

AQA GCSE Biology • Foundation Tier • Paper 2

Foundation Tier Paper 2 📚 21 Subtopics

📋 Topic Overview

Explore how characteristics are passed from one generation to the next, how variation occurs, and how evolution leads to new species. This topic covers DNA, genetics, natural selection, and classification. Foundation tier focuses on core concepts and essential understanding.

🎯 Foundation Tier Focus

Foundation tier focuses on core knowledge, basic understanding, and straightforward applications. You'll need to recall facts, describe processes, and perform basic calculations.
