📋 Topic Overview

Study the relationships between organisms and their environment. This topic covers ecosystems, biodiversity, human impacts on the environment, and sustainable management of resources. Foundation tier focuses on core concepts and essential understanding.

🎯 Foundation Tier Focus

Foundation tier focuses on core knowledge, basic understanding, and straightforward applications. You'll need to recall facts, describe processes, and perform basic calculations.
