Bioenergetics

AQA GCSE Biology • Foundation Tier • Paper 1

Foundation Tier Paper 1 📚 8 Subtopics

📋 Topic Overview

Discover how organisms obtain and use energy through photosynthesis and respiration. This topic explains how plants make food and how all organisms release energy from food. Foundation tier focuses on core concepts and essential understanding.

🎯 Foundation Tier Focus

Foundation tier focuses on core knowledge, basic understanding, and straightforward applications. You'll need to recall facts, describe processes, and perform basic calculations.
