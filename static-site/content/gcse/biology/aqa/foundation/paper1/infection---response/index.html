Infection & Response

AQA GCSE Biology • Foundation Tier • Paper 1

Foundation Tier Paper 1 📚 13 Subtopics

📋 Topic Overview

Learn about pathogens, how they cause disease, and how our bodies defend against them. This topic includes vaccination, antibiotics, and the development of new drugs to combat infectious diseases. Foundation tier focuses on core concepts and essential understanding.

🎯 Foundation Tier Focus

Foundation tier focuses on core knowledge, basic understanding, and straightforward applications. You'll need to recall facts, describe processes, and perform basic calculations.
