Cell Biology

AQA GCSE Biology • Foundation Tier • Paper 1

Foundation Tier Paper 1 📚 18 Subtopics

📋 Topic Overview

This topic covers the fundamental unit of life - the cell. You'll learn about different types of cells, cell structures, and how substances move in and out of cells. Understanding cell biology is essential for all other biology topics. Foundation tier focuses on core concepts and essential understanding.

🎯 Foundation Tier Focus

Foundation tier focuses on core knowledge, basic understanding, and straightforward applications. You'll need to recall facts, describe processes, and perform basic calculations.
