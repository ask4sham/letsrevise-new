Higher Tier Paper 2 📚 17 Subtopics

📋 Topic Overview

Understand how organisms maintain stable internal conditions and respond to their environment. This includes the nervous system, hormones, plant responses, and human reproduction. Higher tier students will explore more complex concepts and applications.

🎯 Higher Tier Focus

Higher tier requires deeper understanding, application of knowledge to unfamiliar contexts, and more complex calculations. You'll need to analyze data, evaluate information, and explain complex biological processes.
